Road Infrastructure Investment Trusts (InvITs) are emerging as a key instrument for financing and managing road projects. These InvITs allow investors to pool their funds into infrastructure assets, offering stable returns and fostering sectoral development. RI 360 tool aims to provide comprehensive performance analysis of all road InvITs in India, delivering insights into investment trends, risk assessments and profitability metrics. The platform leverages real-time data and advanced analysis to empower stakeholders and investors in making informed decisions.
